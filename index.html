    FAQs

    What is full stack development?

    Full stack development refers to the development of both the front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) portions of a web application. It involves working with various technologies and frameworks to create a complete and functional application.

    What skills are required to become a full stack developer?

    To become a full stack developer, you should have proficiency in both front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as back-end technologies like Node.js, Python, or Java. Additionally, knowledge of databases, version control systems, and server management is beneficial.

    How long does it take to complete the full stack development course?

    The duration of the course depends on the learning pace of the individual student. However, typically our full stack development course can be completed within 4 to 5 months.

    Do I need any prior coding experience to enroll in the full stack development course?

    While prior coding experience is helpful, it's not mandatory. Our course is designed to accommodate beginners as well as those with some programming background. We provide comprehensive lessons and hands-on projects to help you grasp the concepts regardless of your starting point.
